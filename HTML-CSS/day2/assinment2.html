About guva fruit cultivation

Guava is grown in both tropical and sub-tropical regions upto 1,500 m. ... Heavy clay to very light sandy soils having pH between 4.5-8.2 are suitable for cultivation of guava. Good quality guavas are produced in river basins. The crop is sensitive to water-logging. Guavas are tropical trees originating in Central America. Their fruits are oval in shape with light green or yellow skin and contain edible seeds. What's more, guava leaves are used as an herbal tea and the leaf extract as a supplement.
